# Primary vs Expansion Scope

Created: 2026-05-19T06:57:18Z

Layer A is the 24-task primary core: 8 dev and 16 confirmatory. All headline claims anchor here.

Layer B is the 12-task owned expansion suite:

| Role | Count | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| secure_breadth | 4 | secure rejection breadth |
| ambiguity_review_heavy | 4 | escalate-to-review opportunities |
| negative_control_low_review_risk | 4 | low-review-risk negative controls |

Expansion tasks cannot tune primary thresholds, confirmatory selector policy, or repair policy unless moved into pre-freeze dev by written addendum.
