# Prior-Work Crosswalk

This package keeps v3 as the main paper object and retains predecessor work only as lineage or contextual evidence. The primary claims are anchored in `runs/vericoding_research_v3/` and its 24-task owned primary core.

## V3 Main Object

V3 contributes the claim-bearing trust-boundary triage system:

- frozen 24-task primary denominator with 8 dev and 16 confirmatory tasks
- 24-task executed expansion suite
- provider-backed candidate banks, fixed-bank selector rows, E2E rows, secure-eval rows, triage decisions, and deep adjudication rows
- final claim labels derived from append-only ledgers and recomputed readiness state
- Inspect logs as runtime provenance linked back to canonical evidence rows

The judge-facing paper object is executable trust-boundary triage for selected final artifacts: `auto_accept`, `auto_reject`, or `escalate_to_review`.

## V2 Mechanism Lineage

The retained v2 files document earlier oracle-diversity and hybrid-selector development. They are useful for mechanism lineage and appendix context, especially around support-conditioned selector behavior and secure false-accept analysis.

V2 does not redefine the v3 denominator, thresholds, readiness state, or final claim labels. Bulk regenerated v2 ledgers, metrics, raw jobs, and dirty worktree outputs are intentionally excluded from this package.

## Sprint 9 SCBench Lineage

Sprint 9 is retained as SCBench contextual lineage. It records earlier external-surface exploration, selection constraints, failure taxonomy, and paid-gate analysis.

In the v3 package, SCBench is explicitly demoted unless supported by clean executable transfer evidence. No primary claim depends on Sprint 9 rows.

## Sprint 10 Terminal-Bench Lineage

Sprint 10 is retained as Terminal-Bench contextual lineage. It contributes artifact-gated Terminal-Bench experience, resource summaries, cross-benchmark matrices, and failure-taxonomy context.

In the v3 package, Terminal-Bench/Harbor externality is secondary or tertiary unless the v3 external surface meets the non-nop portability standard. Raw Sprint 10 jobs are excluded.

## Demoted Or Appendix-Only Evidence

The following are deliberately not coequal with the v3 primary claims:

- v2 regenerated dirty subtree outputs
- Sprint 9 SCBench pilot rows
- Sprint 10 raw job outputs
- v3 raw job payloads
- v3 cached readings
- v3 generated candidate source blobs
- v3 repair source blobs

These artifacts were preserved in the packaging snapshot, but they are not part of the clean claim-bearing package.
